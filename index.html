Selected Work

Side Project · AI · SaaS

Signal — Feedback Intelligence

Signal transforms raw product feedback into structured roadmap work. Slack messages and GitHub issues are ingested in real time, scored for sentiment and urgency using AI models, clustered into emerging themes, and converted into structured product epics ready for tools like Linear or Jira.

Side Project · AI · Fintech

Prism — Portfolio Intelligence

Prism is an AI-assisted portfolio intelligence engine for Canadian investors. A deterministic optimisation model evaluates potential portfolio actions and surfaces the single highest-expected-value move. Investors can then explore the reasoning through natural-language follow-up questions.

The Lab

The Lab is where I explore ideas in artificial intelligence, finance, and product systems. Most of these begin as small prototypes, thought experiments, or technical investigations into how intelligent software could improve decision-making.

01

Product Feedback Intelligence

Exploring how LLM-based pipelines can transform unstructured product feedback into structured insights. This includes experiments in signal ingestion, sentiment scoring, pattern detection, and converting qualitative feedback into opportunities product teams can act on.

02

Financial Decision Engines

Research into deterministic optimisation systems for personal finance and investing. I’m interested in how algorithms can evaluate portfolio actions such as rebalancing, tax-loss harvesting, account optimisation, and capital deployment in a transparent and explainable way.

03

AI Evaluation Systems

Investigating how organisations can benchmark AI models before deploying them into financial workflows. Focus areas include reasoning accuracy, hallucination detection, financial calculation reliability, and structured evaluation environments for decision-support systems.

04

Geopolitical Signal Monitoring

Experimenting with systems that aggregate geopolitical events, market signals, and macroeconomic developments into clearer intelligence for analysts and investors. As part of a team, I contributed to World Monitor, an open-source OSINT application (GitHub). With a background in international studies and diplomacy, I find the intersection of structured intelligence and technology fascinating.

About

Product manager exploring how AI changes decision-making.

I’m interested in the gap between what technology could do for people and what it actually does. My career has moved through three domains that all revolve around data, systems, and decisions: financial markets, digital health, and AI-driven investing tools.

I began working in fintech in London across FX, payments, and trading platforms before moving into digital health in Canada, where I worked on connected healthcare systems and clinical data platforms.

Today I work on the future of self-directed investing at CIBC Investor’s Edge, focusing on personalisation systems, AI-assisted product features, and intelligent client journeys.

How do we turn complex data into decisions humans can trust?

Outside work, I enjoy golf, cooking Sunday roasts, following the rugby, and renovating a house in Toronto with my wife Natalie.
